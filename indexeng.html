Un Réveil Mouvementé

S2 project of the ANAT team

Why are we developing a game?

We are four students at EPITA Toulouse, and this school requires the realization of a group project over a period of 6 months, with a presentation at the end of the year. As we got along well, we decided to form the ANAT group, and to develop our own video game. This is how "Un réveil mouvementé" was born, and we hope with all our heart that our project will give you a good time.

Plot of the adventure

Our game features 2D puzzle, action and adventure gameplay. When the game starts, you wake up on a beach, without knowing where you are. Soon, you will have to collect information and solve puzzles to progress. After learning about the place where you have landed, you will be confronted with different monsters and traps that await you in the dark...
